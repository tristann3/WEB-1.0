Uncharted is an action-adventure game series developed by Naughty Dog and published by Sony Interactive Entertainment for PlayStation consoles. The main series of games follows Nathan Drake, a treasure hunter who travels across the world to uncover various historical mysteries.

This game raised the standards of single-player video games, praising it for quality storytelling, gameplay mechanics, character design, realistic graphics, and delivering an enjoyable cinematic experience.

Naughty Dog is the developers behind some of the most iconic games in the evolution of Playstation

  • Crash Bandicoot

    1996 - 2001

    Crash Bandicoot was produced by Naughty Dog for only 5 years before selling it to Sony, where all went downhill from there. But before they sold it they made this platformer which players control the protagonist Crash who traverses several levels in order to progress.

  • Jak and Daxter

    2001 - 2004

    This was a revolutionary platformer in the early 2000s. From the first game's guided open world story, to the last games graphical improvements, This game series is considered a classic for the Playstation 2.

  • Last of Us

    2013 - 2020

    The Last of Us was nothing short of greatness. In this post apocalyptic Story Mode, every decision you make will effect you down the road. With brilliant graphics and a well curated story line, The Last of Us is Naughty Dog latest and greatest.
